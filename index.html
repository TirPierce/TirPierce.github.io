Welcome to Dublin Ink

Dublin Ink pride ourselves on offering a comfortable open and friendly environment to be tattooed in, understanding how intimidating tattoo studios can be…(we scare ourselves)!Our studio is an open creative space which can not only guarantee you a great tattoo, but also a great experience.
